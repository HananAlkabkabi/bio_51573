## Curriculum Vitae

# Hanan Alkabkabi  
Ph.D. Candidate | Plant Genomics & Bioinformatics Researcher  

Ph.D. candidate with a strong background in cell and molecular biology, bioinformatics, and data-driven genomics research. My work focuses on genome-wide association studies (GWAS), genomic prediction, and computational approaches for plant breeding and stress tolerance analysis. I integrate statistical modeling, bioinformatics pipelines, and machine learning techniques to translate genomic insights into applied breeding strategies.
